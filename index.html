The Winter Fey

The Winter Fey is a short, festive, & winter-themed one-shot adventure for 10th level adventurers, set in and around the tiny isolated mountain hamlet of Brisque.

Adventure Overview

You and your companions have received word from Frankie Crackers a friend of a friend, about the passing away of an old associate, comrade, partner, family member, or advocate, Parkie Smiles the talented and cheeky bard with a heart of gold and more coin owed than owned.
Frankie Crackers has gotten in touch with you to help her track down the mythical Winter Fey- a benevolent and wish-granting world spirit. The myth goes that spirit visits a small village but once a year upon the Night of Hearths to grant but a single wish. With months of research, Frankie has decided that Brisque is that village and so the spirit itself must make it's home nearby.

She wants to track down the Winter Fey before the Night of Hearths and secure the single wish for Parkie's resurrection.

Character Creation & Starting Equipment

A link to the DnD Beyond Campaign can be found HERE!

This is an adventure designed for 10th level Adventurers, the only restrictions on character creation are that the characters must be created using officially licensed books (Not Unearthed Arcana, sorry!). Content sharing has been enabled, so you should have access to all currently released D&D content on DnD Beyond.

All races and classes are available & multiclassing is allowed!

Character Creation

Stat generation - To generate each stat, roll 4d6, and drop the lowest number rolled.
Placement of Stats - Your stats can be assigned anywhere after all six numbers have been generated.
Restarting - After racial modifiers have been applied, check to see if you have at least two stats over fifteen (+2), if not, then you may re-roll your stats.
Backstory - Backstory can be anything that you like, as long as a few things remain true-
1. You have reason to see Parkie Smiles resurrection through- you might be her friend, an ex-lover, a stalker, a debtor, whatever!
2. Your character has never been to Brisque before, nor met Frankie Crackers. And that's it!

Starting Gold - Each Character starts with 5000 gold.

Equipment & Gear - All characters will begin with their normal starting equipment based on their class, background, etc, but as 10th level adventurers you can use the 5000 starting gold to equip your character as you see fit, using the prices listed in the links below.

Armour & Shields, Weapons, Adventuring Gear, Artisans Tools.
